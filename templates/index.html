ALYNE CHAT

Description and Features

AlyneChat is a revolutionary decentralized communication platform designed to keep you connected to friends and family when the traditional world goes dark. By leveraging advanced P2P mesh networking, AlyneChat transforms your smartphone into an independent node, allowing for seamless chatting without the need for Wi-Fi, cellular data, or expensive satellite plans. Whether you are navigating a crowded festival, hiking in the deep wilderness, or traveling internationally, you can maintain your private circles without relying on a central provider. What truly sets AlyneChat apart is its ability to facilitate long-distance, oversea offline communication. Through a sophisticated multi-hop relay system, your messages securely "hop" across a network of devices, bridging vast distances and even maritime gaps to reach loved ones across the water. In times of crisis, the app serves as a critical lifeline with its integrated Emergency SOS feature. This allows you to broadcast high-priority distress signals and location data to the entire mesh network during infrastructure outages or natural disasters. Built on a foundation of absolute privacy and decentralization, AlyneChat ensures your data is never stored on a server. It is more than just an app; it is a resilient, secure, and borderless connection to the people who matter most.

Required Dependencies :

ALYNENET module
WiFi - Direct
Bluetooth
Android Ver.11+
